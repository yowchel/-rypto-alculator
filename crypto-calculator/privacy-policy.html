Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 20, 2025

Introduction

Crypto Calculator ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Crypto Calculator is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data to external servers.

Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Selected Cryptocurrencies: Your chosen cryptocurrencies for calculation
  • Theme Preference: Your light/dark mode setting
  • Language Preference: Your selected app language

This data is stored only on your device using AsyncStorage and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

CoinGecko API

We use the CoinGecko API to fetch current cryptocurrency prices. When the app requests price data, your IP address may be visible to CoinGecko as part of standard web requests. We recommend reviewing CoinGecko's Privacy Policy.

React Native Community NetInfo

We use @react-native-community/netinfo to check your network connection status. This check is performed locally on your device and does not transmit any data.

Data Security

Since all user preferences are stored locally on your device, the security of this data depends on your device's security settings. We recommend:

  • Using device encryption
  • Setting a strong device passcode
  • Keeping your operating system up to date

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages to use.

Data Retention and Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all app data by:

  • Uninstalling the app from your device, or
  • Clearing app data through your device settings

Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store your personal data on external servers, there is no data for us to:

  • Access
  • Correct
  • Delete
  • Transfer

You have complete control over your local data through your device settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

International Users

Since we don't collect or transmit personal data, there are no international data transfer concerns. All data remains on your device regardless of your location.
